Hundred Islands National Park Tour

Hundred Islands National Park is located in the northern Philippines in the province of Pangasinan. There are 124 pristine coral reef islands scattered across an area of 7.12 square miles (18.44 sq km).

Bolinao Tour

Bolinao is dubbed the Boracay of Pangasinan due to its powdery white sand beaches and coconut trees planted along the coast of Patar beach. 

Pangasinan Churches Pilgrimage Tour

Pangasinan has a lot of churches, such as the Minor Basilica of Our Lady of the Rosary of Manaoag. Pangasinan is known for its strong Catholic history.
