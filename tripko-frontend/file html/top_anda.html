Top Attractions in Anda

Tanduyong Island

a small island featuring a wide white sandy beach and abundant tropical vegetation. Located just one kilometre from Tondol Beach, this island can be reached on foot at low tide. For tourists, Tanduyong Island is a peaceful place with stunning sea landscapes and clear waters that surround the island.

Panacalan Island

From Tondol Beach in Anda, you can take a boat to Panacalan Island for about 30 minutes. You will be enchanted by the panorama of the Panacalan coast with clear waters and fine white sandy beaches.
