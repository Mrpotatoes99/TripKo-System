Top Attractions in Bani

Surip Beach

The Beach has crystal clear seawater and it is ideal for swimming. It has a thriving marine ecosystem, making it ideal for snorkeling and scuba diving. Stunning view of some limestone rock formations and mountains surrounding the beautiful beach are great backdrops for taking selfies and groupies.

Bani Hidden Paradise Beach Resort

Bani-Hidden Paradise Beach Resort is now a popular getaway in Bani Pangasinan. Strategically located on the edge of the mountainside of Brgy. Dacap Sur, Bani Pangasinan. Having beautiful scenery, pristine white sand beach, creative garden landscapes, and refreshing ambiance.

Polipol Island

Polipol Island is one of the most popular tourist destinations Bani Pangasinan. Amuse yourself with nature, Polipol Island has lots of stunning scenery. It is also a perfect spot for sunset watching. This little paradise became popular because of its natural beauty and its cliff jumping activities.
